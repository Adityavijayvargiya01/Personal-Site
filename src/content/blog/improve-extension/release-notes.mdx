- **Right-click to improve**: Select any text on a webpage, right-click, and choose "Improve Text" - it's that simple!
    - Selected text automatically appears when you open the extension
    - Get a notification confirming your text was captured
    - Works on any website
- **Smoother animations**: Better visual feedback throughout the extension

How the new right-click feature works:

1. Select text on any webpage
2. Right-click and choose "Improve Text"
3. Click the extension icon
4. Your text automatically loads and starts improving!
